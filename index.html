POMOARC
v1.0.0

Stripped-back focus timer. Three colors. No noise.

How It Works

  • Focus for 25 minutes. One orange arc. One countdown.
  • Hydrate for 5 minutes. Step away. Drink water.
  • Rest for 20 minutes after four cycles. Reset.

Under the Hood

  • State-isolated rendering. Zero stutter at 60fps.
  • SwiftData persistence. All data lives on-device.
  • GPU-offloaded arc via drawingGroup().
  • Low Power Mode aware. Shadows and glow disabled automatically.

Privacy

Pomoarc does not phone home. No analytics, no accounts, no cloud sync. Your session history is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.
